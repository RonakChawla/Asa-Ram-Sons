Why choose us?

Reliable Supply Chain

Collaborative Growth

About Us

The firm was started by Mr. Satish Chawla in the year 1999. The firm has grown manifold since then.

Mr. Satish had been working in the biscuits manufacturing and managing production and distribution for 15 years before starting this firm.

Having experience in the complete value chain has been extremely valuable in helping understand the requirements of various stakeholders.

We strive to fulfill the requirements of our brands and distributors with all our efforts. This has helped us build a reputed business all over Himachal.

  • 35+ years total experience in the industry.
  • 22+ years experience in distribution network.
  • We specialize in Bakery (Biscuits, Cakes, Rusks), Confectionary, Beverages, Namkeen & Snacks verticals but also have experience in Sauces, Pickles, Soap, Detergent verticals.
  • We are also open to expand our portfolio in other verticals for reputed brands.
